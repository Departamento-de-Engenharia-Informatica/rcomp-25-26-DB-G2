### Notes

- Horizontal links use CAT7 copper with a maximum length of 90 m per channel; an average of 40 m per link was assumed for length estimation.

- **Patch panels and rack sizing** use **24‑port = 1U** and **48‑port = 2U**, total panel space **S**, required space **4 × S**, and standard **48U** racks. See **`../README.md`** for the full **Telecommunication Enclosure Sizes** table for Room 4 (IC + HC0).
